# ADR-0004 — Discovery Outcome: Proceed to Build

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 2026-05-06  
**Author:** Ali (CPO)

---

## Decision

The team is skipping the structured 14-day discovery sprint and proceeding directly to MVP build.

## Rationale

- The founding team are the first users — the pain is self-evident.
- Saudi-specific cap table pain (SJSC entity type, Companies Law 2023, ZATCA exports) is well-understood by the team from direct experience.
- Speed of iteration > discovery ceremony at this stage. Real product in front of real customers faster than a structured sprint allows.
- Discovery happens in parallel with building through design partners and early customer feedback.

## Consequences

- Sprint 1 begins immediately.
- Customer validation is ongoing, not front-loaded.
- Scope changes driven by early user feedback are expected and welcome.
- The implementation plan in `docs/product/implementation-plan.md` is now the active plan.
