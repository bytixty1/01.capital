# Cap Table & Corporate Law Glossary
# مسرد مصطلحات جدول الملكية والقانون التجاري

> Bilingual reference. Used in product copy, customer interviews, contracts, and team conversations. Always check this glossary before inventing a translation — Saudi legal Arabic has established conventions that should not be casually replaced.

---

## A — Entities & Structures

| English | Arabic | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Limited Liability Company (LLC) | شركة ذات مسؤولية محدودة | Most common Saudi corporate form |
| Joint Stock Company (JSC) | شركة مساهمة | Vehicle for larger / public companies |
| Simplified Joint Stock Company (SJSC) | شركة مساهمة مبسطة | New form for startups / VC; no minimum capital |
| Single-shareholder company | شركة الشخص الواحد | Solo-founder company; permitted under New Law |
| Family business | شركة عائلية | May have binding family charter |
| Articles of Association | عقد التأسيس / النظام الأساسي | Constitutional document; varies by entity type |
| Articles of Incorporation | عقد التأسيس | Used for SJSC and single-person LLCs |
| Bylaws | اللائحة الداخلية | Internal governance rules |
| Commercial Registry | السجل التجاري | Maintained by Ministry of Commerce |
| Commercial Registration (CR) | السجل التجاري | Company registration certificate |

## B — Capital & Shares

| English | Arabic | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Capital | رأس المال | Total contributed value |
| Share / Shares | سهم / أسهم | Used for JSC and SJSC |
| Partner interest | حصة | LLC equivalent; not called "share" |
| Shareholder | مساهم | JSC/SJSC owner |
| Partner | شريك | LLC owner |
| Common share | سهم عادي | Standard voting share |
| Preferred share | سهم ممتاز | With economic preference, often non-voting |
| Treasury share | سهم خزينة | Company-held own shares |
| Ownership percentage | نسبة الملكية | % of capital owned |
| Voting percentage | نسبة التصويت | % of voting rights — may differ from ownership |
| Profit allocation | توزيع الأرباح | May differ from ownership per AoA |
| Capital increase | زيادة رأس المال | Issuance of new shares |
| Capital decrease | تخفيض رأس المال | Reduction of capital |
| Bonus shares | أسهم مجانية | Issued via capitalization of reserves |
| Cash contribution | حصة نقدية | Cash paid in for shares |
| In-kind contribution | حصة عينية | Non-cash assets contributed |
| Share certificate | شهادة سهم | For JSC; not issued in LLCs |
| Partner register | سجل الشركاء | LLC ownership record |
| Shareholder register | سجل المساهمين | JSC/SJSC ownership record |
| Share class | فئة الأسهم | Common, preferred series, etc. |

## C — Employee Equity

| English | Arabic | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Employee Share Plan (ESOP) | برنامج أسهم الموظفين | Generic term for employee equity programs |
| Employee Stock Option | خيار شراء أسهم للموظف | Right to purchase at strike price |
| Vesting | استحقاق | Earning right to shares over time |
| Vesting schedule | جدول الاستحقاق | Time-based earning structure |
| Cliff vesting | استحقاق دفعة واحدة بعد فترة | All vests at once after the cliff period |
| Graded / ratable vesting | استحقاق متدرج | Vests in installments |
| Strike price / Exercise price | سعر التنفيذ / سعر الممارسة | Price to exercise an option |
| Grant date | تاريخ المنح | When option/share is granted |
| Vesting commencement date | تاريخ بدء الاستحقاق | Often equals grant date or hire date |
| Cliff period | فترة الانتظار | Period before any vesting (typically 1 year) |
| Exercise / Exercise option | تنفيذ الخيار | Convert option to actual shares |
| Phantom share | سهم وهمي / حصة وهمية | Cash-settled equivalent |
| RSU (Restricted Stock Unit) | وحدة سهم مقيدة | Shares granted with vesting |
| Buyback / Repurchase | إعادة الشراء | Company repurchases shares from departing employees |
| Forfeiture | المصادرة / الإسقاط | Loss of unvested options on departure |
| Exempted offer | عرض مستثنى | ESOP grants typically qualify |

## D — Investment & Convertibles

| English | Arabic | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Convertible note | سند قابل للتحويل | Debt that converts to equity |
| Sukuk | صك / صكوك | Sharia-compliant debt instrument |
| Sukuk-convertible | صك قابل للتحويل | Sharia-compliant convertible equivalent |
| SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) | اتفاقية بسيطة للأسهم المستقبلية | Common in early-stage US startups; less common in Saudi |
| Pre-money valuation | التقييم قبل الاستثمار | Company value before investment |
| Post-money valuation | التقييم بعد الاستثمار | Company value after investment |
| Discount rate | نسبة الخصم | Conversion discount on convertibles |
| Valuation cap | سقف التقييم | Maximum conversion valuation |
| Funding round / Series | جولة تمويل / سلسلة | Series A, B, C, etc. |
| Term sheet | ورقة الشروط | Non-binding outline of investment terms |
| Subscription agreement | اتفاقية الاكتتاب | Binding agreement to purchase shares |
| Shareholders agreement | اتفاقية المساهمين | Governs shareholder relations |

## E — Rights & Restrictions

| English | Arabic | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Pre-emptive right | حق الأولوية في الاكتتاب | Existing shareholders get first right to new issuances |
| Right of First Refusal (ROFR) | حق الأولوية في الشراء | Existing partners can match third-party offers |
| Drag-along right | حق إلزام بالبيع | Majority can force minority to sell |
| Tag-along right | حق المرافقة | Minority can join majority's sale |
| Anti-dilution | الحماية من التخفيف | Protection against dilutive issuances |
| Liquidation preference | أفضلية التصفية | Priority in payout on exit |
| Vesting acceleration | تسريع الاستحقاق | Vesting speeds up on triggers (e.g., acquisition) |
| Pledge | رهن | Using shares as collateral |
| Lock-up period | فترة الحظر | Restriction on share transfers |
| Transfer restriction | قيد على التحويل | Limitation on share transfers |

| English | Arabic | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Board of Directors | مجلس الإدارة | JSC governance body |
| Board manager | مدير الشركة | LLC equivalent of board |
| General Assembly | الجمعية العامة | Shareholder/partner meeting |
| Ordinary General Assembly | الجمعية العامة العادية | Annual / standard meeting |
| Extraordinary General Assembly (EGA) | الجمعية العامة غير العادية | Major decisions: capital changes, ESOPs, AoA changes |

| Resolution | قرار | Formal decision |
| Board resolution | قرار مجلس الإدارة | Decision by directors |
| Shareholder resolution | قرار المساهمين | Decision by shareholders |
| Voting threshold | نسبة التصويت المطلوبة | Required % to pass resolution |
| Family business charter | ميثاق الأسرة / ميثاق الشركة العائلية | Binding inter-family governance document |
